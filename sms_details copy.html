SMS Marketing

Short Message Service (SMS) marketing is one of the cost-effective strategy for marketing. Businesses just starting and looking for a cost-effective advertising option will find it a worthwhile marketing strategy with its friendly pricing. Most of the consumers prefer to receive promotions through text messaging and more than 90% of messages are read in the first 90 seconds. So undoubtedly, it is a great opportunity to deepen relationships with the customers. Only a basic cell phone and a cellular network is required for a text message. It is safe to say that all the active cell phones support text messaging. SMS is a unique and effective way for promoting a company or business organization, its products, services, events as well as the marketing campaigns via SMS.

Types of Solutions

Bulk SMS Service: It is undoubtedly one of the best features which delivers thousands of SMS in seconds.
API SMS Service: API provides a powerful yet easy to send SMS Capability to a project. Add SMS functionality to School Management Software, Small Business, ISP, Corporates & NGO in Bangladesh.
Promotional SMS Service: Promotional SMS messages are texts you’d send to your audience to increase sales, promote a product, or build brand awareness.
Masking SMS: Number Masking is a great branding tool and also servers to identify yourself to your mobile recipients. Through Masking SMS service text message are sent to the receiver with containing brand or business name.
Non-Masking SMS: It delivers a message to the receiver in a random number.
